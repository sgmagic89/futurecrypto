Future Crypto is an open source, global payment network that is fully decentralized without any central authorities. Mathematics secures the network and empowers individuals to control their own finances. Why use future crypto?

Digital currency will only work if there is a distributed network for it to run on. This requires users to adopt the coin and want to use it. You also need merchants who will accept the coin as a form of payment, and finally you need developers who will create tools, API’s and help bridge the gap to make it easy and practical to use.

Who can use it?

INDIVIDUALS

Anybody can start using Future Crypto to transfer money to friends, family, or to pay for goods and services. Future Crypto gives you complete control over your money because YOU are the only one who can transfer it from your wallet to another wallet. Without your private key nobody can touch your Future Crypto , which makes it very safe and secure. You can also stake your and earn interest for helping support the network.

MERCHANTS

Any business in the world can start accepting Future Crypto for their goods and services. Future Crypto gives merchants more options and flexibility to accept payments from all over the world. Future Crypto is safe, cheap, and easy to use with no charge backs, low fraud and no compliance hoops or banks to deal with. We are also launching a complete plug and play merchant platform that will make Future Crypto even easier to accept.

Developers

Calling all Developers! Future Crypto is open source and we are looking for more developers to contribute to our platform. We are building API’s that will help with exchanges, wallets, and anything else making the coin easier to use. Future Crypto is ideal for micro payments. With the strength of Future Crypto Network members we believe this coin will be adopted very easily and the ecosystem will be thriving. We would love to have you part of it!
